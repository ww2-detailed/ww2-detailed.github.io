Second World War

Overview

The Second World War, shortened as WW2 (World War 2) or the Second Great War in contrast to the Great War, was a global conflict between the Axis and the Allies. Nearly all of the world participated by April of 1945. This was the deadliest war so far in recorded history, surpassing the Great War and even the Taiping Rebellion.
